The University of Georgia has received a $5 million grant aimed at improving K-12
science education across the state. The multi-year initiative focuses on building a
sustainable network to recognize, train, and retain high-performing secondary science
educators.

The funding targets the teacher pipeline rather than classroom materials alone, with
the goal of keeping strong science teachers in Georgia schools over the long term.

Investing in science teachers is upstream of everything this site works toward: strong
