In February 2026, nearly 600 educators gathered in Macon for the annual Georgia Science Teacher Association conference, built around the theme of taking science beyond the lab. The conference offers professional development workshops and a venue for teachers to share classroom resources and strategies. GSTA, an organization of roughly 1,000 members, partners with the Georgia Department of Education and presents over $14,000 in grants and awards to students and teachers across the state each year.

Read the [primary source](https://www.wpganews.com/2026/02/13/georgia-science-teachers-seek-more-funding-classroom-resources/).

Events like this support the science educators whose work underpins scientific literacy across Georgia.
